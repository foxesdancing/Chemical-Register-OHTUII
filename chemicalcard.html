TEHOKAS CLEANSPRAY 500 ML

ID: FR-09

Description

Chemicals MUST be marked using standard clauses as a template.

Use Purpose

Industrial cleaning spray for removing grease and dirt.

Chemical Formula

C₃H₈O

Safe Storage

Store in a cool, well-ventilated place away from heat and open flames.

Hazard Phrases

  • H222: Extremely flammable aerosol
  • H229: Pressurized container may burst if heated
  • H319: Causes serious eye irritation

Precautionary Phrases

  • P210: Keep away from heat/sparks/open flames
  • P305+P351+P338: IF IN EYES: Rinse cautiously with water

Hazard Pictograms

Flammable

Irritant

First Aid Measures

  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air immediately.
  • Ingestion: Do not induce vomiting. Seek medical help.
  • Skin Contact: Wash with soap and water.
  • Eye Contact: Rinse with water for several minutes.
